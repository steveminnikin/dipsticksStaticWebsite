Tank gauging tapes for dipping all types of storage tanks

Description

A high quality carbon steel dipping tape for where a dipstick cannot be used.

Used on its own it will provide a depth of liquid in a tank but when used in tandem with a strapping table can accurately measure the volume of liquid in a tank.

Specifications

  • Construction: Lightweight aluminium frame winder. High impact plastic handle. Complete with graduated brass dip weight
  • Units: Black etched with raised markings. Graduated in mm on one side, with feet, inches and 1/16ths on the reverse
  • Lengths: Available in 10, 20 & 30m versions
  • Accuracy: To EC Class II standard
  • Suitable For: General purpose tank dipping. Use with a Strapping Table to convert height readings in to a volume.
For Use In
Combination with a Dip Tape or uncalibrated dipstick to give volume readings
Calibration of other tank gauging systems
