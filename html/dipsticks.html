Description

Our dipsticks are manufactured from T section GRP making them durable yet flexible and therefore ideally suited for use in demanding environments

The dip rod can be calibrated to the exact dimensions of your storage tank meaning that they provide highly accurate liquid volume measurements at a cost-effective price

We can also provide dipsticks marked at regular height intervals rather than volumes, such as every 10 millimetres.

Specifications

  • Material: Specially formulated GRP mixture ensuring the correct electrical and thermal properties in accordance with BSI regulations.
  • Lengths: From 1m up to 5m.
  • Measurements: To suit your needs: litres, cubic metres, gallons US gallons. Or regular height intervals: millimetres/inches.
  • Suitable For: All non-consumption liquids: oil, diesel (DERV), petrol, paraffin, kerosene, hydraulic fluid.
  • Customisable: We can engrave your dipstick to your requirements, such as: tank reference, telephone number etc.
